The central chain

Alkalai and Kalischer → Hibbat Zion and Mohilever → Mizrachi and Reines → Rav Kook → Fishman Maimon and the State of Israel

This chain shows the gradual movement from longing and religious thought to settlement, organization, spiritual interpretation and state responsibility.

Main connections

  • Alkalai and Kalischer: transformed longing for Zion into a call for practical preparation.
  • Kalischer and Mohilever: connected the idea of settlement with real colonies and public action.
  • Mohilever and Reines: moved from rabbinic support for settlement to organized Religious Zionist politics.
  • Reines and Rav Kook: combined organizational structure with deep spiritual interpretation.
  • Rav Kook and Maimon: connected the meaning of national revival with public leadership in the state.
