How to use this page

This page lists categories of sources for further research: primary writings, historical studies, encyclopedias, archives and image repositories.

General sources

Studies on early Zionist thought, Hibbat Zion, the First Aliyah, Mizrachi and Religious Zionist ideology.

Alkalai and Kalischer

Primary writings such as works on practical redemption, settlement and the return to Zion.

Mohilever and Hibbat Zion

Research on the colonies, the shmita controversy, Baron Rothschild and rabbinic support for settlement.

Reines and Mizrachi

Sources on the founding of Mizrachi, religious education and cooperation with political Zionism.

Rav Kook

Works such as Orot, Orot HaTeshuva and Igrot HaRa’ayah, alongside research on his philosophy.

Fishman Maimon

Sources on Mizrachi in Eretz Israel, the Yishuv, the Declaration of Independence and the Ministry of Religions.

Images

Wikimedia Commons, the National Library of Israel and the Central Zionist Archives are useful for image research and copyright checking.
