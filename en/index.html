What the site explores

This website presents the emergence of Religious Zionism as a gradual historical process. It follows the movement from religious longing for Zion to practical action, settlement, organized political activity, spiritual interpretation, and finally participation in the building of the State of Israel.

The focus is not only on dates and events, but also on ideas: redemption and human initiative, Torah and national revival, settlement and public responsibility, education, leadership, and the religious meaning of Jewish sovereignty.
